# Privacy Policy

This is a small technical site. The goal is to collect as little personal data as practical.

## Server logs

The hosting platform may collect standard request logs such as IP address, user agent, requested URL, referrer, and timestamp for security, debugging, and abuse prevention.

## Analytics

No analytics provider is intentionally configured in this draft unless added later. If analytics are added, this page should be updated to identify the provider and what it collects.

## Ads and affiliate links

Display ads and affiliate links may be added later. Those services may use cookies, tracking parameters, or similar technologies subject to their own policies. This page should be updated before ads go live.

## Contact

For privacy questions or corrections, use the repository issue tracker: [github.com/sudoflux/sudoflux.io](https://github.com/sudoflux/sudoflux.io).
